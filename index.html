Hello.

In year 2013 my adventure begins. I was born in huge meadows with a bit of forest and even small lake in the middle. For almost 5 years I lived there with my pony-family. After that new life started. But even if now I have my own box in stable - I still feel like real wild mustang inside.

My mum is arabian mare named Karina and my dad is Dasti Man, a welsh pony stallion. Here is where my name - Kasti - come from. Combination of those two. My breed is called just "pony" or "small horse" in Poland as I am not pure arabian or welsh.
